Feature: FNOL Intake and Claim Processing
  As a Claims Adjuster at FMNE,
  I want to capture FNOL submissions via portal, call center, or mobile
  So that claims are initialized with correct coverage verification, ISO fraud checks, and appropriate reserve workflows.
  Background:
    Given the claims adjuster is authenticated in the FNOL system
    And the adjuster has access to the claim intake and processing features
  Scenario: Successful FNOL submission via portal
    And a new home insurance claim is initiated
    When the incident date "2024-02-10" is specified
    And the policy number "HOM-2024-XYZ789" is provided
    And the damage description "Water damage from burst pipe" is documented
    And the required photos and plumber's report are uploaded
    And the claim submission is completed
    Then the claim is registered in the system
    And the claim number "CLM-2024-987654" is assigned
    And the confirmation message "Your claim has been submitted successfully" appears
    And a confirmation email is sent to the policyholder
  Scenario: FNOL submission with missing policy number
    And a new auto insurance claim is initiated
    When the incident date "2024-03-05" is specified
    And the damage description "Side swipe on highway" is documented
    And the required photos are uploaded
    And the claim submission is attempted
    Then the claim submission fails
    And the error message "Policy number is required" is displayed
    And the claim is not registered in the system
  Scenario: FNOL submission via call center with fraud alert
    And a new travel insurance claim is initiated
    When the incident date "2024-04-20" is specified
    And the policy number "TRV-2024-DEF456" is provided
    And the incident description "Lost luggage at airport" is documented
    And the claim submission is completed
    Then the claim is registered in the system
    And the claim number "CLM-2024-123456" is assigned
    And a fraud alert is triggered for further investigation
    And the adjuster is notified of the fraud alert
  Scenario: FNOL submission via mobile with incorrect date
    And a new health insurance claim is initiated
    When the incident date "2025-01-01" is specified
    And the policy number "HLT-2024-GHI789" is provided
    And the treatment description "Emergency room visit for injury" is documented
    And the claim submission is attempted
    Then the claim submission fails
    And the error message "Incident date cannot be in the future" is displayed
    And the claim is not registered in the system
